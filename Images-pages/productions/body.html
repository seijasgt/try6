Off the grid
A short 8 minute documentary about MAARA, a permaculture farm located in Nova Friburgo, Rio de Janeiro, Brazil.
Their goal is to become a reference and permaculture playground for like-minded individuals. They seek to learn alongside others, share their experiences, produce their own food, and enhance the fertility, productivity, and biodiversity of the land. They are committed to learning how to live in harmony and respect with nature and the surrounding community.
I had the opportunity to stay there 30 days in which I shot and edited this documentary
